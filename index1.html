USB Type-A is the most common USB connector. It features a rectangular shape with four pins inside.

USB Type-B is a square-shaped connector used mainly for peripherals like printers and scanners.

USB Type-C is a versatile and compact connector with a symmetrical design, meaning it can be plugged in either way.

USB Lightning is Apple's proprietary connector used primarily for iPhones, iPads, and iPods.
